1. What keyword is used in Java to indicate that the value of a variable
   will not change(i.e., the variable is actually a constant) ?
=  final

2. What is the Java convention for naming constants ?
=  using uppercase and underscore if it has more than 1 word.

3. Name of advantage of creating names for constants.
=  Inconsistencies and typographical errors are less likely.

4. Why is TWENTY_FOUR not a good name for the constant 24?
=  because it could be means something that's not 24, it could 
   be an hour in a day, or it could be the date you born, date
   that's probably your favourite day and so on.
